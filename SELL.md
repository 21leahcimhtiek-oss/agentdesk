## Value Propositions

### For DevOps / Platform Teams
- Deploy AI agents in minutes, not weeks
- Full observability: logs, costs, success rates per agent
- Webhook-driven automation integrates with existing tools (PagerDuty, Slack, Jira)
- Role-based access keeps production agents protected

### For AI-First Companies
- Model-agnostic (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini, GPT-3.5-turbo today; extensible)
- Template library reduces time-to-first-value
- Per-agent cost tracking makes FinOps straightforward
- API-first — embed in your own products

### For Enterprises
- SSO / SAML for zero-friction identity management
- Audit logs for SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance
- Dedicated support with SLA
- Unlimited agents and team members
