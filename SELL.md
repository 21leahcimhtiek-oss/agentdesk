# AgentDesk — Sales Playbook

## Value Proposition
AgentDesk solves the #1 pain point for teams shipping AI agents: **zero visibility into what your agents are actually doing in production**.

## Target Customers
- Engineering teams shipping LLM-powered products
- AI-first startups scaling agent workloads
- Enterprise AI platform teams managing multiple agent deployments

## Objection Handling
- "We can build this ourselves" → Time-to-value: AgentDesk deploys in minutes. Building this takes 3+ months.
- "Too expensive" → One prevented incident pays for a year of AgentDesk.
- "We use LangSmith/Weights & Biases" → AgentDesk is agent-agnostic and multi-tenant from day one.

## Competitive Advantages
1. Multi-tenant from day one — perfect for agencies and platform teams
2. Cost tracking with hard budget limits and alerts
3. GPT-4o powered failure analysis — not just logs, actual root cause
4. Webhook alerts to existing Slack/PagerDuty workflows
